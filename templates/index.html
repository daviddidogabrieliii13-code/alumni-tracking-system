About WVSU Pototan

Bridging Education to Career Excellence

Western Visayas State University - Pototan Campus has been a beacon of quality education in the region for decades. Our Alumni Tracking and Graduate Tracer Study System represents our commitment to continuous improvement and our dedication to our students' success.

By tracking our graduates' career paths and gathering their feedback, we can better understand how our education translates into real-world success and identify areas for improvement in our curriculum.
